AI tenant screening sounds opaque — black box in, verdict out. Here's exactly how Squatter Away works, step by step, so you know what happens between "send link" and "get report."

## Step 1: Create a screening link

You generate a secure, single-use link from your Squatter Away dashboard. Send it to your applicant by email, SMS, or your preferred channel. The link opens an encrypted intake form — no app download required.

## Step 2: Applicant submits documents

Your applicant provides:

- Government-issued ID
- Employment details and contact information
- Bank statements (typically 2–3 months)
- Social profile references (optional but recommended)
- Rental history and references

Everything submits through encrypted channels. Data is encrypted at rest and access-scoped to your account.

## Step 3: AI agent begins verification

Once submission is complete, our AI underwriting agent activates. Unlike a human reviewer working through a checklist, the agent:

- **Extracts structured data** from unstructured documents (PDF pay stubs, bank statements)
- **Validates employment** against authoritative sources
- **Analyzes bank patterns** — income consistency, obligations, NSF events
- **Cross-references identity** across all submitted documents
- **Reviews social footprint** for identity matches and public red flags
- **Flags inconsistencies** between any signals

Each verification runs in parallel, not sequentially — which is why turnaround is hours, not days.

## Step 4: Cross-referencing and synthesis

Individual signals are useful. Cross-referenced signals are decisive. The agent checks:

- Do bank deposits match stated income and pay stubs?
- Does employment tenure match the application narrative?
- Does the ID match social profile identity markers?
- Are there unexplained gaps or contradictions?

The output isn't 12 separate pass/fail results — it's a synthesized assessment with reasoning.

## Step 5: Report delivery

You receive a decision-ready report containing:

- Overall recommendation (approve, review, deny)
- Signal-by-signal breakdown with evidence
- Income analysis and rent-to-income ratio
- Identified red flags and inconsistencies
- Documentation suitable for audit and fair-housing defense

Most reports deliver within an hour. Edge cases requiring human review typically resolve within 24 hours.

## What AI doesn't do

- Make the final leasing decision (you do)
- Guarantee a tenant will pay rent (nothing can)
- Replace your compliance obligations (FCRA, fair housing — still yours)

AI handles the verification grunt work so you focus on judgment.
